About us

I am an expat living in Switzerland. My family is still back in Argentina, almost 14.000kms away. During my first year here, they wanted to send me a gift on my birthday, something that they could send directly to my door by post. But between the language differences and the ignorance of how this foreign country works, they never made it. And that's how 'Breakfast at Switzerland's' came to life. An user-friendly, expat-proof company that takes care of the production, preparation and delivery of an universal gift: a nice, warm and delicious breakfast.
